      How do I play?

      Arrow keys move (D-pad). Prefer a mouse or touch? Once a game is running, turn on the on-screen controller from the controls below the screen. To see or remap every button for keyboard or gamepad, open the game's own menu from its control bar. On phones and tablets, EmulatorJS's touch controls also appear automatically.

      Want a retro CRT look? While a game is running, open its menu from the control bar and go to Settings - built-in Shaders (CRT, scanlines and more) are in there.
