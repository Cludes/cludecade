Browser Game Boy

Runs entirely in your browser. Your ROM never leaves your device.

Play retro games in your browser

Drag & drop a ROM here, or click to browse. Supported: Game Boy (.gb), Game Boy Color (.gbc), Game Boy Advance (.gba), NES (.nes), SNES (.sfc/.smc), and zipped ROMs (.zip)

How do I play?

Arrow keys move (D-pad). Prefer a mouse or touch? Once a game is running, turn on the on-screen joystick (with A, B, Start and Select) from the controls below the screen. To see or remap every button for keyboard or gamepad, open the game's own menu from its control bar. On phones and tablets, EmulatorJS's touch controls also appear automatically.
